About the Remake

This is a remake that gives a distinct emotional feel, offering a sense of a sad and darker somber atmosphere, all wrapped in a cute package.

Meet Our Directors

We'll bring in Tim Burton as the director, adding his unique gothic flair, while David Lanzenberg will handle the editing, ensuring a polished final product.

Our Concept

Imagine if Tim Burton directed a Rambo movie with a Wednesday Addams vibe, but everything had an undercurrent of kawaii. The jungle transforms into a gothic, maze-like nightmare, filled with traps and eerie shadows, yet sprinkled with unexpectedly cute elements.

The Setting

Picture an old hospital, now hidden by a growing forest, covered in thick vines and trees. Once bustling, it's now a quiet, rusty building with walls falling apart, being reclaimed by nature. Yet, amidst the decay, you might spot a family of adorable woodland creatures making it their home.

Production Magic

We're using cutting-edge technology to bring our vision to life. Adobe Photoshop will craft our still posters with precision, while Premiere Pro will add motion and excitement to our animated versions. The result? A perfect blend of professional quality and irresistible charm.
